# MTG IQ – Support MTG IQ is a quiz app that challenges you to identify Magic: The Gathering cards by their artwork. ## Features - Identify cards by art crop - Guess card name, artist, or both - Progressive hints and fuzzy matching - Quiz modes with scoring and streaks - Track stats and accuracy over time ## Data Sources Card data provided by Scryfall.
